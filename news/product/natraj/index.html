Natraj: AR + AI Powered Anatomy Learning Platform

Revolutionizing anatomy education with real-time AR body scans, interactive 3D models, and an AI tutor that transforms traditional medical learning into an engaging, accessible, and clinically accurate experience.

By Navchetna EdTech Team • 10 min read
Try Now

Overview

Natraj represents a paradigm shift in medical education. Traditional methods textbooks, cadavers, and 2D diagrams are valuable but often lack interactivity and personalization.

This platform introduces real-time AR visualization and an AI-powered tutor, enabling students and educators to explore anatomy directly on the human body. Learners can ask questions and receive medically accurate explanations instantly. By bridging theoretical study with practical understanding, Natraj makes anatomy education immersive, interactive, and future-ready.

Key Innovation

Immersive AR overlays and AI-driven tutoring that provide interactive, clinically accurate insights tailored to students' needs.

New Features

Skeletal System (Phase 1)

  • AR Body Scan: Overlay skeletal structures in real time
  • 3D Bone Visualization: Explore accurate anatomical proportions
  • Interactive Labels: Tap to reveal names, functions, and clinical notes
  • AI Q&A: Instant answers on anatomy and pathology

Fracture & Pathology Simulation (Phase 2)

  • Realistic fracture demonstrations
  • Animated healing stage overlays
  • Pediatric vs. adult anatomy comparisons

Advanced Learning Capabilities (Phase 5)

  • AI Tutor: Guided lessons, quizzes, and explanations
  • Virtual Reality Mode: Full-body immersive simulations
  • Surgical Training Simulations: Safe practice modules for professionals
