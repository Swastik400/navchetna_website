Abstract

Plaur 1.0 represents a paradigm shift in the domain of artificial intelligence-driven automation. While most contemporary tools specialize in either workflow automation or content creation, Plaur unifies these domains into a singular, intelligent framework. By combining large-scale workflow orchestration, prompt-based intent recognition, and multi-modal content generation, Plaur establishes itself as an all-in-one solution for individuals, enterprises, and communities.

This paper outlines the conceptual foundation, technical innovations, and empirical benchmarks of Plaur 1.0, situating it within the broader context of intelligent automation and next-generation digital ecosystems.

Introduction

The exponential growth of digital operations across industries has amplified the need for intelligent systems capable of executing workflows, managing data, and generating content with minimal human intervention. Traditional platforms such as robotic process automation (RPA) tools, standalone content generators, and isolated task managers are limited by their narrow focus and fragmented architectures.

Plaur 1.0 addresses this challenge by integrating automation, workflow orchestration, and AI creativity into a seamless ecosystem. Developed in collaboration between Navchetna (NINELLMS Solutions LLP) and Tantrix Solutions OPC Pvt Ltd, Plaur offers an intent-driven system where a single prompt can trigger multi-stage processes — from enterprise-scale automation pipelines to real-time social media deployment.

Key Contributions

Unified Automation Engine: A single system capable of integrating APIs, databases, and enterprise workflows.

Multi-Modal Content Creation: Text, images, campaigns, and digital collateral generated contextually and tailored to brand identity.

Prompt-to-Execution Paradigm: Natural language prompts directly translated into structured actions.

Scalable Architecture: Capable of handling concurrent workflows across departments and organizations.

Enterprise-Grade Reliability: Compliance-ready, encrypted, and supported by fault-tolerant infrastructure.

System Architecture
1. Universal Automation Core

Plaur’s automation core supports:

End-to-end workflows without coding.

API integrations with cloud services, business tools, and data systems.

Dynamic triggers and scheduling to support adaptive business logic.

2. Content Intelligence Layer

Built upon proprietary LLMs and creative generation engines, this layer enables:

Automatic generation of blogs, social posts, campaign materials, and emails.

Brand-aware visual generation for marketing.

Adaptive messaging that aligns with context and audience.

3. Prompt-to-Action Framework

Plaur introduces a Prompt-Oriented Execution Model (POEM):

A natural language prompt is parsed into an intent tree.

The system maps the intent to workflows and services.

Tasks are executed autonomously, with optional human approval stages.
